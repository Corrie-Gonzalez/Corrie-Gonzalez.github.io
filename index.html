Hi, I’m Corrie

I’m an ecologist specializing in geospatial analysis, ecological modeling, and interactive mapping. My work focuses on using spatial data to better understand and manage natural systems. I also build tools that help transform large biological and environmental datasets into clear, useful insights for conservation.

I’m especially interested in riparian ecosystems, species distribution modeling, and applied conservation science.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my husband and my dogs, hiking, yoga, being active, and spending time outdoors.
