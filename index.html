Welcome!

I’m an ecologist specializing in geospatial analysis, ecological modeling, and interactive mapping. My work focuses on using data to better understand and manage natural systems. I also build spatial tools that help translate complex environmental and biological data into clear, actionable insights for conservation. I am partiulcarly interested in riparian ecosystems, species distribution modeling, and conservation ecology.
